Switching to Online

If you would like to go back online, simply go to http://www.coursesmart.com when you have an internet connection. You will be taken to the CourseSmart home page. Sign In and you will be all set to work online.

Checking in eTextbook

You only need to check in your eTextbook if :

  • You would like to access your eTextbook offline on another computer.
  • You would like a refund on your eTextbook, you must check in your eTextbook to be eligible for a refund.
  • If you have checked out more than 5 eTextbooks. You can have up to 5 eTextbooks offline at one time. We'll prompt you to check an eTextbook back in when you've reached the 5 book limit.

To Check in your eTextbook:

  • With an internet connection, log into www.coursemart.com.
  • Go to My BookShelf and find your book.
  • Click on the Manage Offline Book button.
  • Click Check In Book.

Highlighting Text in an eTextbook

To highlight a passage, do the following:

  • Click and drag your mouse over the section of text you wish to highlight.
  • Click the Highlight button in the toolbar.

To remove highlighting, hold your mouse over the highlighted section and click the X that appears in the left margin of the text.

You can see a list of all pages that contain highlighted text by clicking the Highlights tab in the panel to the left of your eTextbook page.

Highlights in your offline and online eTextbook copies will automatically synchronize when you go online and sign in to your CourseSmart account. You do not need to check in your eTextbook to sync.

Adding Notes to an eTextbook

You can add your own comments and annotations to your eTextbook.

To add a note, do the following:

  • From the page that you would like to annotate, click the Add Note button on the Navigation Bar of your Reader.
  • A note window will appear over your eTextbook page.
  • Enter a title that will help you identify the Note later.
  • Type your note in the Notes field.
  • Click the Save button.

To access your note later:

  • Click on the Notes tab in the margin at the left of the page.
  • Specify whether you want to see all Notes or only the notes attached to the current page.
  • From the list displayed, select the note you are interested in. It will open on top of the page you are currently viewing, but you can use the Go to this page link to go to the page the note refers to. Or click View Detailed Notes to see the full text of all of your Notes.

To remove a note, open the Note and click the Delete button.

Notes in your offline and online eTextbook copies will automatically synchronize when you go online and sign in to your CourseSmart account. You do not need to check in your eTextbook to sync.

Copying Text from an eTextbook

To copy text from an eTextbook do the following:

  • Click and drag to select the text that you wish to copy.
  • Click on the Copy button on the CourseSmart Reader toolbar.
  • The text you have selected will appear in a small window.
  • Highlight this text and use your usual copy/paste commands or shortcuts to copy the text and paste it into a document of your choice.
  • Click the red x in the corner of the Copy window to close it.

This operation removes the text formatting, so it may not appear the way it does in your eTextbook.

Printing Pages from your eTextbook

To print pages from your eTextbook, you must be online. You cannot print pages from your eTextbook offline.
